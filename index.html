About me

I am a final-year PhD candidate at the Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Wuyang Hu.

My research focuses on building sensing systems and infrastructure for passive and continuous in-home health monitoring (computational screening and surveillance, smart aging), spanning across various disciplines, including wireless sensing, cyber-physical systems, embedded systems, ubiquitous computing, and machine learning.

I am on academic job market this year, please reach out for any opportunities!
